About Saflec Systems

Our Story

Saflec Systems is a trusted name in the access control industry, known for delivering reliable, high-performance hardware and software solutions. Proudly designed and manufactured in South Africa, our products reflect decades of technical expertise and innovation from a team of highly skilled engineers and developers.

As part of the Saflec Group, we collaborate closely with our sister company, Saflec, specialists in advanced metal detection technologies. Together, we offer comprehensive security and detection solutions tailored to a wide range of industries.
